## Why Choose Us

Coast Resuscitation Group is a local, family-owned business providing high-quality resuscitation training for clinicians across the Gold Coast and Northern NSW. Our courses are practical, engaging, and designed to build real confidence in emergencies.

What sets us apart is our strong connection to the local community. As active members of Rainbow Bay Surf Life Saving Club, we bring a genuine commitment to service, teamwork, and ongoing clinical education. This community focus shapes the way we teach and the supportive learning environment we create.

When you train with us, you learn from instructors with extensive clinical backgrounds who understand the realities of frontline care. We keep our sessions hands-on, relevant, and centred around the skills clinicians use most in practice.

Learning here also comes with the relaxed coastal lifestyle that the Gold Coast and Northern NSW are known for - beaches, cafés, and a training environment people actually enjoy being in.

## About Us

Coast Resuscitation Group was founded by two Registered Nurses from Northern NSW with experience in critical care, paediatrics, maternity and clinical education.

Alongside raising a young family, we are actively involved in surf lifesaving at Rainbow Bay SLSC, which reflects our commitment to community, safety, and education.

We established Coast Resuscitation Group so clinicians could access high-quality ALS training close to home, without the need to travel long distances or attend large, impersonal courses. Our values - professionalism, kindness, respect, and genuine care - guide everything we do, from how we run our workshops to how we support each learner.

Our aim is simple: deliver training that is practical, evidence-based, and personalised, ensuring every participant leaves feeling more capable and confident.
