        <h2>Course Overview</h2>
        <p>The Trauma Nursing Core Course (TNCC) is regarded globally as the benchmark program for developing trauma nursing capability. Grounded in the latest evidence and designed by the Emergency Nurses Association (ENA), TNCC equips clinicians with a structured, reliable framework for rapidly identifying and managing life-threatening injuries in the crucial first minutes of trauma care.</p>
        <p>Across the 9th Edition curriculum, participants learn through a blend of interactive discussions, visual demonstrations, immersive skill stations and realistic scenarios that reflect contemporary trauma presentations. TNCC strengthens clinical thinking, prioritisation and teamwork, enabling nurses to deliver safe, timely and coordinated trauma care in any setting.</p>
