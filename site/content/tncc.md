# Trauma Nursing Core Course (TNCC)

**Duration**: 2 days  
**Time**: 08:00am – 04:30pm  
**CPD Hours**: 10 hours  
**Catering**: Morning tea, lunch and afternoon tea provided  
**Expiry**: 4 years

## Course Description

The Trauma Nursing Core Course (TNCC) is a two-day intensive program for nurses focusing on systematic trauma assessment and early intervention. Through expert instruction, hands-on simulations, and evidence-based content, participants enhance critical thinking and clinical skills to deliver high-quality trauma care across diverse patient populations and emergency situations.

## Learning Outcomes

- Apply a systematic approach to the initial assessment and management of trauma patients using the A–J mnemonic.
- Identify and prioritize life-threatening injuries requiring immediate intervention.
- Demonstrate effective airway management and ventilation techniques in trauma scenarios.
- Recognize the signs and stages of shock and implement appropriate resuscitation measures.
- Assess and manage head, spinal, thoracic, abdominal, musculoskeletal, and burn trauma.
- Adapt trauma care strategies for special populations, including paediatric, geriatric, obese, pregnant, and LGBTQ+ patients.
- Collaborate effectively within an interdisciplinary trauma team to ensure safe, coordinated care.
- Integrate psychosocial and interpersonal considerations into trauma nursing practice.
- Utilize evidence-based guidelines to guide decision-making in emergency and disaster situations.
- Evaluate patient outcomes and reflect on the effectiveness of trauma care interventions.

## Assessment Requirements

Successful completion of the course includes scoring 80% or greater on the multiple choice examination and demonstrating all critical steps and 70% of the total points in the evaluated skill station – the Trauma Nursing Process.

## Who Should Attend

- Registered Nurses
- Emergency Department Nurses
- Critical Care Nurses
- Trauma Nurses
- Nurses working in emergency or trauma settings
- Nurses seeking to enhance their trauma care capabilities
